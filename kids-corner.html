AO Kids Corner – Episode 2: Grandma Moves In

Welcome Back, Little Explorers!

Today’s story is all about family, surprises, and learning how to share your space with someone new. Let’s jump in!

Scene 1 – A Big Surprise

Mom: “Kids! Guess what? Grandma is coming to stay with us for a while!”

The kids froze. Their eyes got big. Their brains started spinning like a blender on turbo mode.

Jay: “Wait… like… stay? Here? In the house?”

Mom: “Yes! Isn’t that exciting?”

Jay and Lila looked at each other. They loved Grandma… but she had rules. Lots of rules.

Scene 2 – Grandma Arrives

The front door swung open and in came Grandma with a suitcase, a giant smile, and enough snacks to feed a small village.

Grandma: “Hello, my sweet babies!”

She gave the biggest hugs in the universe — the kind that squeeze the air out of your lungs but also fill your heart.

Lila: “Grandma… are you staying in my room?”

Grandma: “Only until we set up the guest room, darling.”

Lila nodded. Sharing was hard… but she could try.

Scene 3 – New Routines

Grandma woke up early. Very early. Before-the-sun early.

She made oatmeal. She opened curtains. She hummed old songs. She reorganized the kitchen — twice.

Jay: “Grandma… where are the cereal bowls?”

Grandma: “Top shelf! Better for posture!”

Jay sighed. This was going to take some getting used to.

Scene 4 – A Heart-to-Heart

That night, Grandma sat with the kids on the couch.

Grandma: “I know things feel different. But I’m so happy to be here with you.”

Lila: “We’re happy too… it’s just… new.”

Jay: “Yeah. But we’ll figure it out.”

Grandma smiled and pulled them close.

Grandma: “That’s what families do.”

Scene 5 – A Cozy Ending

They all curled up under a blanket and watched a movie together.

Grandma fell asleep halfway through — snoring softly like a tiny, friendly bear.

The kids giggled. Maybe this wouldn’t be so bad after all.

The End
